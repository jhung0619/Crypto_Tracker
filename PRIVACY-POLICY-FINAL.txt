Privacy Policy for Crypto Tracker

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

OVERVIEW

Crypto Tracker ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices.

DATA COLLECTION

We DO NOT collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app does not:
• Require user accounts
• Track your location
• Access your contacts
• Store browsing history
• Use analytics tools
• Implement advertising SDKs
• Collect device identifiers
• Store any user data

THIRD-PARTY DATA SOURCES

The app fetches public market data from:

1. CoinGecko (https://www.coingecko.com)
   Purpose: Cryptocurrency prices and market data
   Data accessed: Public market information only
   No personal information is sent to this service

2. Alternative.me (https://alternative.me)
   Purpose: Market sentiment index (Fear & Greed Index)
   Data accessed: Public sentiment data only
   No personal information is sent to this service

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We do not control how they handle network requests.

INTERNET PERMISSION

The app requires internet access to fetch real-time cryptocurrency market data. This is the only system permission required.

INFORMATION DISPLAYED

All information shown in the app is publicly available market data. The app displays:
• Current cryptocurrency prices
• Historical price data
• Market sentiment indicators
• Technical analysis signals
• Public news items

None of this information is associated with individual users or stored on our servers.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The app is intended for users age 18 and older due to its financial nature. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

FINANCIAL DISCLAIMER

Market signals and data displayed in the app are for informational purposes only and do not constitute financial advice. The signals shown (BEARISH/NEUTRAL/BULLISH) are based on technical indicators and should not be interpreted as investment recommendations.

Always consult a licensed financial advisor before making investment decisions. Cryptocurrency investments carry significant risk.

DATA SECURITY

Since we do not collect or store any user data, there is no user data to secure. The app simply displays publicly available information fetched in real-time from third-party APIs.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
