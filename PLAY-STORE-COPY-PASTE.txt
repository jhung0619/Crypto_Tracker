FULL DESCRIPTION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Crypto Tracker provides real-time cryptocurrency market data and insights for Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and Solana (SOL).

FEATURES

• Real-time price tracking
  Live prices updated every minute from CoinGecko

• 30-day price trend charts
  Interactive bar charts showing daily price movements
  Tap any bar to see exact historical prices

• Moving averages (30/90/240/365 days)
  Track long-term trends with multiple timeframes
  Identify support and resistance levels

• Market sentiment indicators
  Fear & Greed Index from Alternative.me
  Understand overall market psychology

• Market signals
  BEARISH/NEUTRAL/BULLISH analysis
  Based on price trends and moving averages
  
• Latest crypto news
  Stay updated with market developments

WHO IS THIS FOR?

Perfect for crypto investors and traders who want quick market insights on mobile. Whether you're checking prices during the day or analyzing trends before making decisions, Crypto Tracker gives you the essential data you need.

DISCLAIMER

⚠️ IMPORTANT: This app provides market data and informational signals only. 

• Not financial advice
• Not investment recommendations
• Not a trading platform
• Always do your own research
• Consult a licensed financial advisor before making investment decisions

Signals shown (BEARISH/NEUTRAL/BULLISH) are based on technical indicators and should not be interpreted as buy or sell recommendations.

DATA SOURCES

• Price data: CoinGecko public API
• Sentiment data: Alternative.me Fear & Greed Index
• News: Crypto market aggregators

All data is publicly available and provided as-is.

REQUIREMENTS

• Internet connection required
• Android 5.1 or higher
• No account needed
• Free forever

PRIVACY

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app only fetches public market data from third-party APIs. No tracking, no analytics, no data collection.
